AirGuide uses an admin-approved registration flow. You submit your details, wait for an administrator to review your account, and then log in once approved.

## Creating an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the registration page">
    From the map at `/`, click **Iniciar Sesión** in the top-right corner, then click **Regístrate aquí** at the bottom of the login form. You can also navigate directly to `/register`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Complete all four fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Nombre Completo** | Your full name |
    | **Correo Electrónico** | Your institutional email address |
    | **Matrícula** | Your student ID number |
    | **Contraseña** | A password of at least 8 characters |

    Confirm your password in the **Confirmar Contraseña** field. The form validates that both passwords match before submitting.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Crear Cuenta**. If the email or student ID is already registered, you will see an error. On success, a confirmation message appears on the page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for admin approval">
    Your account is created with a `pendiente` (pending) status. You cannot log in until an administrator reviews and approves your account.

    <Note>
      There is no automated notification when your account is approved. Contact your campus administrator if you need to follow up on your approval.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account states

Every student account has one of three states:

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `pendiente` | Newly registered — awaiting admin review |
| `activo` | Approved — you can log in and use all features |
| `rechazado` | Rejected — login is not permitted |

<Warning>
  If your account is in `rechazado` state, the login form will show "Credenciales incorrectas o usuario no activo". Contact your campus administrator to understand why your account was rejected.
</Warning>

## Logging in

Once your account is `activo`, go to `/login` and enter your institutional email and password.

- **Admins** are redirected to `/admin/dashboard` after login.
- **Students** (`alumno` role) are redirected to the map at `/`.

Your session is stored in `localStorage` under the keys `token` (JWT) and `usuario` (your profile object). The session persists across browser refreshes until you log out.

<Info>
  Attempting to log in with a `pendiente` or `rechazado` account will fail with "Credenciales incorrectas o usuario no activo", even if you entered the correct password.
</Info>

## Logging out

Click **Cerrar Sesión** in the top-right header bar of the map. This clears your session from `localStorage` and redirects you to `/login`.
